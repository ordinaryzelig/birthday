This year for my birthday,
you will probably die.

On Saturday October 26, we will play Blood on the Clocktower, a social deduction game where you are secretly either good, or evil, or drunk, or poisoned, or all of the above. No matter your alignment or mental state, the odds are not in your favor to survive the night. A demon in human form lives among the townsfolk. With the help of secret minions, the demon can kill anybody in their sleep, and the townsfolk can vote to execute anyone just for their “suss vibes”. Team Good wins by democratically executing the demon. Team Evil wins if the demon survives to the end.

As host, I will run the game as the storyteller.
